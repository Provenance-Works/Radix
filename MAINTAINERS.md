## Organization

Radix is maintained by [Provenance Works](https://github.com/provenance-works).

## Responsibilities

Maintainers are expected to:

- Review and merge pull requests within 7 days
- Triage issues within 48 hours
- Ensure zero `sorry` policy is maintained
- Follow the [three-layer architecture](docs/en/architecture/README.md) in all reviews
- Uphold the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)

## Becoming a Maintainer

We welcome contributors who demonstrate:

1. **Consistent, high-quality contributions** — multiple merged PRs with proofs
2. **Understanding of the architecture** — familiarity with Spec → Impl → Bridge layers
3. **Community engagement** — helpful in Discussions, reviews, and issue triage
4. **Lean 4 / Mathlib proficiency** — ability to write and review formal proofs

If you're interested, open a [Discussion](https://github.com/provenance-works/radix/discussions) or reach out to an existing maintainer.

## Decision Process

- **Minor changes** (typos, docs, small fixes): Single maintainer approval
- **New features / theorems**: Lead maintainer approval required
- **Architecture changes**: Lead maintainer + community discussion (RFC)
- **Breaking changes**: Lead maintainer + RFC + minimum 7-day comment period
